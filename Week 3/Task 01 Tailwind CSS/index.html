Welcome to the MCU

Explore the heroes, villains, movies, and shows that shaped the Marvel Cinematic Universe into the greatest saga ever told on screen.

Heroes

From Earth’s mightiest heroes to cosmic guardians, the MCU’s lineup of heroes spans across galaxies.

Iron Man

Tony Stark, a genius billionaire who built his own suit to become Iron Man. His sacrifice helped save the entire universe.

Captain America

Steve Rogers, the super soldier and symbol of hope, who led the Avengers with courage and honor.

Thor

The God of Thunder, who found strength not just in Mjölnir, but in his heart and leadership.

Black Panther

T’Challa, king of Wakanda, who stood for his people and became a global icon of heroism.

Villains

The MCU has given us unforgettable villains who tested the limits of our heroes.

Thanos

The Mad Titan who wielded the Infinity Gauntlet, bringing devastation with a snap.

Loki

God of Mischief, whose cunning plans and complex character blurred the lines between good and evil.

Ultron

An AI born from Stark’s vision, who decided the world could only be saved by eradicating humanity.

Killmonger

Erik Killmonger, a revolutionary with a tragic past, challenged Wakanda’s traditions and leadership.

Movies

Here are some iconic entries in the MCU film saga.

MCU Phases

Phase One

Introduced us to Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, and the Avengers.

Phase Two

Expanded the universe with Guardians of the Galaxy, Ant-Man, and more.

Phase Three

Brought epic team-ups, Black Panther, Spider-Man, and the Infinity Saga’s climax in Endgame.

Phase Four

Introduced the multiverse with Doctor Strange, Spider-Man: No Way Home, and Disney+ shows.

Phase Five

Focused on Kang the Conqueror, with Ant-Man: Quantumania and new Disney+ series.

Phase Six

Set to conclude the Multiverse Saga with Fantastic Four and two Avengers films.

MCU TV Shows

The MCU expanded beyond theaters with Disney+ series.

WandaVision

A unique blend of sitcom and superhero storytelling, exploring Wanda’s grief and powers.

Loki

Following Loki’s escape with the Tesseract, the series introduced the TVA and multiverse concepts.

The Falcon and the Winter Soldier

Explored Sam Wilson’s journey to becoming Captain America and Bucky’s path to redemption.

About the MCU

The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is a shared universe centered on superhero films and series produced by Marvel Studios. Since 2008, it has become the most successful film franchise in history, blending action, drama, and humor across interconnected stories.

Divided into phases, the MCU introduced iconic characters such as Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, and Black Panther, and built toward major events like Avengers: Endgame.

Today, the MCU continues to expand through films and Disney+ series, redefining modern pop culture and inspiring generations of fans around the world.
