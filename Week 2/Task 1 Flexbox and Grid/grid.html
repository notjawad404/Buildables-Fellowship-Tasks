About Formula 1

Formula 1 is the fastest motorsport on the globe. The 2025 season features 24 races across iconic venues—combining cutting-edge technology with human skill, fanfare, and global passion.

2025 F1 Cars

Red Bull RB21

Driven by Max Verstappen & Yuki Tsunoda. Last Honda-powered Red Bull before switching to Red Bull Powertrains-Ford.

Ferrari SF-25

Driven by Lewis Hamilton & Charles Leclerc. A darker red livery with a distinctive white stripe, marking Hamilton's first year with Ferrari.

Aston Martin AMR25

Behind the wheel: Fernando Alonso & Lance Stroll. The fifth Aston Martin car since their 2021 re-entry.

Haas VF-25

Drivers: Esteban Ocon & Oliver Bearman. Features more white elements and special VF-16 tribute livery at Canada.

Mercedes W16

Driven by George Russell & Kimi Antonelli. A new era for Mercedes following Hamilton’s departure; traditional push-rod suspension and refined aero.

McLaren MCL39

Driven by Lando Norris & Oscar Piastri. Reigning Constructors’ Champions, continuing cutting-edge performance with strong early-season testing form.

Alpine A525

Driven by Pierre Gasly & Jack Doohan (rotating with Franco Colapinto). Combines Alpine’s signature pink-blue livery; final year of Renault power after which they’ll switch to Mercedes.

Racing Bulls VCARB 02

Driven by Liam Lawson & Isack Hadjar (with Liam Lawson returning mid-season). Sporting Honda-derived power and white trim; final RBPT-engine year.

Williams FW47

Driven by Carlos Sainz & Alex Albon. Traditional Williams naming; integrated Mercedes systems, showing encouraging midfield pace in testing.

Kick Sauber C45

Driven by Nico Hülkenberg & Gabriel Bortoleto. The final Sauber-Ferrari car before Audi takeover; “C” honoring founder’s wife Christine.

Cadillac F1

Planned debut in 2026. Team backed by GM and TWG Motorsports, led by principal Graeme Lowdon, with motors from Ferrari initially. Drivers confirmed: Sergio Pérez & Valtteri Bottas.
